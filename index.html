GTSAM (Georgia Tech Smoothing and Mapping) is a C++ library that implements sensor fusion for robotics and computer vision applications.

Convex Geometry

I worked with Dr. Galyna Livshyts of the Georgia Tech Math Department to determine the uniqueness of polytopes in n-dimensional Euclidean space given the parameters of face lengths and the set of unit vectors orthogonal to the faces.
